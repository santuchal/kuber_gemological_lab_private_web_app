Ruby Testing

Ruby is famous for the beauty of its red color. Red represents strong emotions like love and anger. The most famous rubies are found in marble, a metamorphic rock. These have rich red color and usually glow a bright red under sunlight. The glow makes the red color even stronger. Darker rubies are found in igneous rock.

The rich colors of Ruby are bluish green to green.

Rubies from Burma have been around since 600 AD. Early Burmese warriors carried rubies to make them invincible in battle. They actually put the rubies under their skin. Ouch!

These real ruby slippers were made to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the movie The Wizard of Oz.
