Emerald Testing

Emerald is the green variety of the mineral beryl. (Beryl also includes aquamarine.) There are small amounts of certain atoms that make the emerald bluish-green to green. The age of the oldest emeralds from South Africa is almost 3 billion years old!

The rich colors of emerald are bluish green to green.

Over 4,000 years ago, most of the ancient emerald jewelry came from the Cleopatra mines in Egypt.

The Atocha Emerald Cross came from a sunken Spanish warship called Nuestra Senora de Atocha.
