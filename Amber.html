Amber is an organic gem. Organic gems come from living or once-living things. Amber formed millions of years ago, when sap from trees hardened and fossilized. Plants and animals were often trapped in this sap. Scientists like to study amber because the gems tell stories of the environment from long ago. Amber is also made into jewellery.

Like a pretty sunset, amber can be orange or golden brown. Like the colors of spring and summer, it is sometimes yellow or green.
